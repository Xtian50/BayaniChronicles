Andres Bonifacio

Father of the Philippine Revolution

Biography

Andres Bonifacio was a revolutionary leader who founded the Katipunan, a movement that sought independence from Spanish colonial rule. Known as the "Father of the Philippine Revolution," his bravery and dedication inspired many to fight for freedom.

His life was marked by fierce patriotism and a relentless commitment to the cause of independence, making him an enduring symbol of the Filipino struggle for liberty.

Legacy

Bonifacio's ideals and actions have continued to influence generations of Filipinos, and his story remains a cornerstone of Philippine history.
